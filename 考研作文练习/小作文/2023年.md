# AI润色
**Notice**

November 4th, 2025

A **research assistant** **is sought to** participate in Professor Smith’s research project **concerning** campus sports activities. Interested individuals are encouraged to apply.

The ideal candidate is required to possess the capability for efficient questionnaire collection and demonstrate proficiency in statistical analysis. Complimentary meals will be provided throughout the project's duration.

Interested candidates should submit their applications via email to Smith@edu.cn no later than November 30th.

## 修改后
**Notice**

November 4th, 2025

A research assistant is sought to participate in Professor Smith’s research project concerning campus sports activities. <u>This initiative（新方案） aims to comprehensively assess student engagement in athletics and identify factors influencing their participation. </u>（项目背景）

<u>The primary responsibilities include distributing questionnaires to diverse student groups and subsequently, performing statistical analysis on the gathered data. </u>（工作内容）Therefore, the ideal candidate is required to possess relevant capabilities and demonstrate proficiency in statistical analysis. Additionally, we anticipate a time commitment of approximately 10 hours per week. Complimentary meals will be provided throughout the project's duration.

<u>This position offers a valuable opportunity to gain hands-on research experience and contribute to the enhancement of campus athletic policies. </u>（工作价值）Those interested should submit their applications via email to Smith@edu.cn no later than November 30th.

Li Ming
