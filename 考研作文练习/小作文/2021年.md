# 题目
A foreign friend of yours has recently graduated from college and intends to find a job in China. Write him/her an email to make some suggestions .
You should write about 100 words on the ANSWER SHEET.
**Do not** sign your own name at the end. Use “Li Ming” instead.
# 初稿
Dear Smith, 

With respect to how to attain a job in China, I feel obliged to share my experience to you. In light of my arduous efforts on seeking a job, three are some advices for your consideration. 

In the first place, bettering your resume serves as a cost-effective approach to be employed. The more time and energy you invest in your profile to highlight your advantages briefly and specifically, the bigger chance that you receive invitation of subsequent reviews. 

Another practical suggestion is improving your communication skills. This endeavor helps you to convert your achievements to stimulating words. 

I remain convinced my suggestion is condusive to you. Should you require further clarification, do not hesitate to contact me. 

Yours sincerely, 
Li Ming
# 修改语法错误
## 修改后
Dear Smith,

I am writing to express my warm welcome to your plan of working in China. **With respect to** how to secure a satisfying job here, I feel obliged to **share my experience with you**. **Based on** my personal experience, here is some **advice** for your consideration.

**In the first place**, polishing your resume **serves as** a cost-effective approach to getting hired. **It is an established truth that** the more effort you invest in highlighting your strengths concisely, **the greater the chance** you will be invited for an interview. **Another practical suggestion is** to improve your communication skills, **which enables** you to **articulate** your achievements more persuasively.

I remain convinced **that** these suggestions will be **conducive** to your job hunting. **Should you require** further assistance, please **do not hesitate** to contact me.

Yours sincerely, 
Li Ming
