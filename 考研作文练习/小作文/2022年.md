# 题目
Write an e-mail to a professor at a British university, **inviting** him/her to organize a team for international innovation contest to be held at your university.
You should write about 100 words on the ANSWER SHEET.
Do not use your own name in the email; use'Li Ming"instead.(10 points)
# 初稿
Dear Professor,

On behalf of my university, it is my distinct honor to invite you to participate in the international innovation contest to be held at my school on June 1st. The contest, themed "creation and innovation", aims to beef up students' skills of innovation and provide them with hands-on experience. You are expected to organize o team in light of your outstanding expertise. You should lead your team to find a practical approach to solve a problem without limitation, and embody your innovation on your product. The contest includes initial sift, followed by presentation. 

Please could you confirm your attendance before December 29th so that we can do some imperative arrangements. Your reply will be my favor. 

Your sincerely, 
Li Ming
