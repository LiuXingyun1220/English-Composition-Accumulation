# 邀请信
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear ..., 

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】写活动的地点时间+邀请</mark>
1. **On behalf of** [the Student Union / our class / the organizing committee], **it is my distinct privilege to cordially（热情友好地） invite you to <u>attend/judge/participate in [Activity: the English Speech Contest]</u>, **which will be held at** [Place] **on** [Date]. **In light of your** [distinguished achievements / outstanding expertise] **in the field of** [Field], **we believe your presence would be an invaluable asset to us.**

2. **I am writing this email to extend a sincere invitation to you to attend** [Activity]. **Knowing that you have a keen interest in** [Topic], **I am convinced that this event will be a wonderful experience for you.**

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】活动详情：目的+流程+职责</mark>
**介绍活动目的**
- **This [activity] is designed to <u>provide a platform for [students] to [showcase their talents]</u> / <u>approach real-world problems in innovative ways</u>.

**介绍流程/安排**
- **Specifically, participants will** [put their heads together to formulate a detailed proposal for urban waste diposal and recycling]. _(具体来说，参与者将聚在一起制定......的详细方案。)_
- **The agenda features** [a keynote speech], **followed by** [a lively group discussion] **and** [a Q&A session].
- **Highlights of the event include** [Activity A] **and** [Activity B], **which will surely** [benefit the participants].

**说明嘉宾职责**
- **We would be honored if you could** [deliver a speech on...] / [serve as a judge for the contest].
- **Specifically, you are expected to** [provide comments on the contestants' performance].

**附件说明**
- **For further details regarding the schedule, please refer to the attached file.**

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】</mark>
**We eagerly await your participation.** **Please could you confirm your availability (or: attendance) before** [Date] **so that we can make necessary arrangements?** **I am looking forward to your favorable reply.**

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部】</mark>
Yours sincerely,
/Kind regards!（给朋友）
[Name]
# 推荐信/介绍信/求职信/申请信
## 推荐信
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear ..., 

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】开篇点题</mark>
- I am writing to share a recommendation that I believe will perfectly match your needs. **If I were forced to single out just one [ideal choice: candidate / destination / masterpiece] for** [Topic: the best tourist attraction / the most suitable candidate], **it would undoubtedly be** [Name: the Forbidden City / Mr. Li].
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】核心推荐理由</mark>
**推荐人物：**(逻辑链：从品质 -> 到能力 -> 到匹配度)
- **My confidence in recommending** [Name] **stems primarily from** his/her outstanding character. He/She **possesses** [品质: unyielding integrity and diligence], which are rare assets in today’s environment.
- **Complementing these personal attributes is** his/her impressive professional capability. **Featuring** [经历: extensive experience in student management], his/her resume proves that he/she is **an impressive problem solver**. **It is this unique combination of** character and competence **that makes him/her ideal for** the position.

---
**推荐地点：**(逻辑链：从整体地位 -> 到历史/自然 -> 到感官体验)
- **What distinguishes** [Place] **from other attractions is that it boasts** [特色: a perfect blend of history and nature]. **Located in** [地点], this spot enjoys a reputation as a "living museum."
- **Once you step into this place, you will find yourself steeped in** [history / nature]. **Walking through** [景点细节], visitors often feel like stepping into a different era. **Adding to its charm is** the local cuisine; the authentic dishes **will surely tantalize your taste buds and make your trip a culinary adventure.**

---
**推荐作品：**(逻辑链：从总体评价 -> 到具体内容 -> 到独特价值)
- **I would go so far as to say that**（我甚至可以说） [Book/Movie] **is a true masterpiece.** I thoroughly enjoyed my time reading it, and it **left a lasting impression on me**.
- **Specifically, the narrative features** [compelling storytelling], **allowing readers to** view the world from a fresh perspective. **Unlike other works that** solely scratch the surface, this one **has a very special spark**—it **captures the reader's heart** by revealing profound truths about [Topic].

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】再次感谢与期待</mark>
- **In light of these reasons, I am convinced that** [Name/Item] **will exceed your expectations.**
- **Please feel free to contact me** should you require further details. **I would be more than happy to expand on my recommendation.**
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部格式】</mark>
Yours sincerely,
Li Hua

# 投诉信
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear Sir or Madam,
/To Whom It May Concern,
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】</mark>
- **I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with** [购买的产品/服务: the electronic dictionary] **I purchased from your website on** [日期]. **Having encountered persistent issues with the item, I am compelled to voice my disappointment and file a formal complaint.**
- **I am writing to convey my deep disappointment regarding the poor quality of** [产品/服务]. **Much to my regret, the product I received failed to meet my expectations and has caused considerable inconvenience.**
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】具体投诉理由</mark>
1. 质量问题
	- **To begin with, the product is defective and not as described.** **When I unpacked it and tried to use it, it did not function properly at all.**
		- **It broke down for no reason** after only two days of use. 
		- **It is not a complete set with one key component missing.**  
		- **The description did not reflect the actual condition of the product accurately.**
2. 服务/态度问题
	1. **Furthermore, the service provided by your staff was inadequate/unsatisfactory.** **So aggressive was the waiter that he even shouted at us when we asked for help.**
		- **The after-sales support let me down, with long wait times and unresolved issues.**
		- The training provided to your customer service team appears insufficient.
3. 迟送货/违约
	- **In addition, the delivery of my order was significantly delayed.** **This constitutes a clear breach of your service commitment,** despite your company's guaranteed 3-day delivery promise.
---
**造成的后果：**
- **This experience has not only exhausted my patience but also disrupted my arranged schedule.** **Consequently, my trust in your brand has been severely shaken.**
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】提出诉求与期限</mark>
**Given the significance of this matter, I strongly urge you to investigate it immediately.**
**To resolve this issue, I demand** [以下诉求任选其一]:
1. **a full refund** regarding this purchase. (全额退款)
2. **a direct replacement** for the defective item. (直接换货)
3. **a formal apology** for the inconvenience caused. (正式道歉)
**I hope you will give due attention to this matter.** **If this issue cannot be resolved satisfactorily within [three working days], I will have no choice but to seek other ways to protect my rights.**
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部格式】</mark>
Yours faithfully,
Li Hua
# 建议信
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear ..., 
/To Whom It May Concern,
>[!Note]
>不认识的人，落款要使用Yours faithfully

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】</mark>（介绍自己）+ 寒暄 + 明确写作目的
**With respect to** [建议的主题: improving your oral English], **I feel obliged to share my perspectives.** **In light of my personal experience, the following proposals are offered for your consideration.** _（关于...，我觉得有义务分享我的观点。结合我的个人经验，现提供以下建议供您参考。）_

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】具体建议</mark>
组合词使用：In the first place, ... -> Another practical / neat suggestion is ... -> What I would like to mention last is ...

**In the first place,** [具体建议: reading original classics] **serves as a highly cost-effective approach to** [目的: expanding your vocabulary]. **It is an established truth that the more** [付出: time and energy] **you invest in this practice, the more** [收获: linguistic competence] **you will eventually harvest.** _（首先，……是实现……的一种极具性价比的方法。众所周知，你在这方面投入的时间精力越多，最终收获的……就越多。）_
**Another practical suggestion is to** [具体建议: participate in social activities].**To be specific, this endeavor necessitates a good command of** [某种技能: interpersonal skills] **to effectively convert** [A: abstract theoretical knowledge] **into** [B: tangible practical ability]. _（另一个实用的建议是……。具体来说，这番努力需要良好的……技能，以便有效地将（抽象的理论知识）转化为（有形的实践能力）。）_ 或者： [[01-形容词、副词#^2742ef|It is highly advisable for you to engage in more hands-on practice, which is the most effective way to convert textbook knowledge into real skills.]]
**What I would like to mention last is the necessity of** [主题词: perseverance]. **There is no universal recipe for** success, **but** strict self-discipline **will undoubtedly pave the way for your progress.** _（我想最后提及的是……的必要性。成功没有万能配方，但严格的自律无疑会为你铺平进步的道路。）_

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】再次感谢与期待</mark>
**I remain convinced that these strategies will enable you to improve upon** [现状]. **Should you require any further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact me at any time.** _（我深信这些策略能让你在……方面有所改进。如果您需要进一步的澄清，请随时联系我。）_
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部】</mark>
Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
