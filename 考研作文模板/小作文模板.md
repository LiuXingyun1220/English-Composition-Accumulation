# 小作文写作概述
## 格式：
1. 顶格写，每写完一部分，空一行。
	![[小作文格式范文.webp]]
2. 作文纸一共24行，空5行，余19行。
	![[小作文内容排布.webp]]
## 字迹
标点：
- 字母后，紧接着打标点；
- 标点后，空一格再写。
# 小作文整体写作思路
## 一、给谁写？
- 上级 or 平辈
- 认识 or 陌生
- 去信 or 回信
## 二、思路
- 进入角色
- 联系常识
- 设定具体
- 礼貌情商
## 三、第一段（四行之内）
### 格式+内容：
```
称呼，

问候
表达情绪（熟人） / 介绍自己（陌生人）
说明目的（按照题目要求的呈现）
```

### 1. 称呼
- Dear Smith, ——平辈；
- Dear Professor Smith, ——长辈，上级，教授；
- Dear all, ——给一个集体写信；
- Dear `member`/`fellow students`, ——给一个具体的集体写信，如：读书会会员；
- Dear Sir or Madam, ——不认识对方，如：投诉信；
- To whom it may concern, （“敬启者”或“致有关人士”）——不认识对方，如：询问信；
## 2. 问候
- 平辈：How's everything going? 
- 长辈：I hope you are doing great!
- 万能（新）：I hope this message finds you well 
- 特别欢迎：Welcome to China!
- 也可连用：Welcome to China, and I hope you are doing great! 
- 对方有喜：How's everything going? Congratulations on your...
## 3. 表达情绪 or 介绍自己
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【熟人】</mark>
- 回信设定：
	I was delighted to receive your letter, in which you mentioned that you would... 
	或：I am so grateful to you for inviting me to attend your..
- 得知喜讯：
	I felt thrilled to know that you had been successfully...(eg:admitted to your dream school).Congratulations!
	或：I felt thrilled when I heard you were doing/you would do sth. 
- 回复询问：I felt very happy to receive your inquiry about.
	或：I felt delighted to know that you are interested in our programme.
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【陌生人】</mark>
- “我是有组织的人”
	I am（不要说 My name is）Li Ming, the coordinator of `the upcoming graduation ceremony of our university` / `the Aiding Rural Primary School project of our school` /` the upcoming international student trip`.
	I am Li Ming, `the president of the students’ union of our university` / `the head of the English association of our school`.
	I am Ming Li, one of the volunteers in the Hope Project Association of our university.
- 我是小透明我还是要说
	I am Li Ming,one of your future students in China.
	I am Li Ming, a staff member in the marketing department.Unfortunately,I...
	I am Li Ming, one of the customers of your online store.
	I am Li Ming, an international student from China.
	I am Li Ming, and I am interested in your project about..
## 4. 说明目的
`I am writing this letter to...`/`The main purpose of this letter is to...`
	- make some suggestions/recommendations about.. 
	- introduce to you sth we/I will do..
	- <mark style="background: #ADCCFFA6;">cordially</mark>（热情友好地） invite you to join in [sth]/to be the judge of... 
	- discuss with you my initial thoughts on...
	- cancel our meeting. Please allow me to explain the reasons.
	-  report to you the progress of my preparations. 
	- share with you my `experience in [sth]` / `ideas on [sth]`.
	-  make a complaint about an unpleasant shopping experience. 
目的句有的时候有两句并列：
	可能a：我要做什么，所以我要你做什么 I will do.., and I would like to invite you to... 
	可能b：根据题意，小作文的目的有两个 The main purposes of this letter are to..., and to...
1. 我校将举办……，我想邀请您……。
	Our school is going to hold a [活动名], so I am writing this email to cordially invite you to participate.
2. 回答之前来信的疑问。
	- I am delighted to know that you are interested in ..., so I am writing...
	- I am delighted to receive your letter inquiring about ..., so I am writing to... 
	- In your letter, you expressed that [ ], so I am writing to provide my idea.
3. 看到贴征集，我来给建议。
	I happened to see the post on the campus website collecting ideas about ..., so I am writing to make some suggestions.
4. 你刚来中国，我想帮助你。
	In order to help you adapt more quickly, I am happy to suggest some..

# 感谢信
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear ..., 

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】表明写作目的</mark>
**I am writing to extend my heartfelt appreciation to you for** [your generous help / the hospitality you showed me]. (我写这封信是为了向你致以衷心的感谢，因为……)
**（Hardly could I believe my luck when I received your letter. ）I would like to take this opportunity to convey my gratitude to you for** [your timely assistance]. (我想借此机会表达我对你的感谢，因为……)

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】具体理由与影响</mark>
你做了什么 -> 产生了什么好结果 -> 如果没有你（反面假设）
1. 强调对方的帮助（强调句）
	**It was your** [kindness / encouragement] **that enabled me to** [overcome the difficulty / adapt to the new life]. (正是你的善意/鼓励，使我能够克服困难/适应新生活。)
2. 描述对方带来的具体影响 (Detail)
	- **Your** [guidance / advice] **has been of great value to me.** (你的指导/建议对我来说价值连城。)
	- **What impressed me most was** [your profound insight / your patience], **which not only** [solved my problem] **but also** [broadened my horizons]. (最让我印象深刻的是你的深刻见解/耐心，这不仅解决了我的问题，还开阔了我的眼界。)
3. 虚拟语气反衬重要性
	- **Were it not for your help, I could hardly have** [finished the task on time]. (要不是因为你的帮助，我几乎不可能按时完成任务。)
	- **Had it not been for your support, I would have been at a loss.** (如果不是因为你的支持，我当时肯定不知所措了。)

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】再次感谢与期待</mark>
1. 表达“无法言表”的感激
	- **Words fail me when I wish to express how much your aid means to me.** (当我想表达你的帮助对我意义多重大时，我感到语言是苍白的。)
	- **My true gratitude is beyond description.** (我真正的感激之情无法用言语形容。)
2. 表达回馈或期待
	- **I sincerely hope that I can have the opportunity to repay your kindness in the near future.** (我真诚地希望在不久的将来有机会回报你的善意。)
	- **I am looking forward to seeing you again.** (期待再次见到你。)
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部】</mark>
Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
# 建议信
As for...,here are some suggestions according to my personal experience/opinions.
First, ...is a cost-effective way of doing.. The more..., the more..
Second,...requires...a good knowledge/command of.. to convert ..into..

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear ..., 
/To Whom It May Concern,
>[!Note]
>不认识的人，落款要使用Yours faithfully

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】</mark>（介绍自己）+ 寒暄 + 明确写作目的
**With respect to** [建议的主题: improving your oral English], **I feel obliged to share my perspectives.** **In light of my personal experience, the following proposals are offered for your consideration.** _（关于...，我觉得有义务分享我的观点。结合我的个人经验，现提供以下建议供您参考。）_

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】具体建议</mark>
组合词使用：In the first place, ... -> Another practical / neat suggestion is ... -> What I would like to mention last is ...

**In the first place,** [具体建议: reading original classics] **serves as a highly cost-effective approach to** [目的: expanding your vocabulary]. **It is an established truth that the more** [付出: time and energy] **you invest in this practice, the more** [收获: linguistic competence] **you will eventually harvest.** _（首先，……是实现……的一种极具性价比的方法。众所周知，你在这方面投入的时间精力越多，最终收获的……就越多。）_
**Another practical suggestion is to** [具体建议: participate in social activities]. **To be specific, this endeavor necessitates a good command of** [某种技能: interpersonal skills] **to effectively convert** [A: abstract theoretical knowledge] **into** [B: tangible practical ability]. _（另一个实用的建议是……。具体来说，这番努力需要良好的……技能，以便有效地将（抽象的理论知识）转化为（有形的实践能力）。）_
**What I would like to mention last is the necessity of** [主题词: perseverance]. **There is no universal recipe for** success, **but** strict self-discipline **will undoubtedly pave the way for your progress.** _（我想最后提及的是……的必要性。成功没有万能配方，但严格的自律无疑会为你铺平进步的道路。）_

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】再次感谢与期待</mark>
**I remain convinced that these strategies will enable you to improve upon** [现状]. **Should you require any further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact me at any time.** _（我深信这些策略能让你在……方面有所改进。如果您需要进一步的澄清，请随时联系我。）_
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部】</mark>
Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
# 推荐信
It is with much enthusiasm that I recommend ...
It is my absolute pleasure to recommend ...
My choice of ... is ...

The ... is truly phenomenal and I thoroughly enjoyyed my time reading / watching it.
It reveals / features ..., allowing me to ...
It teaches me that ... / It makes me feel that ...

... has a very special spark, and it has changed ... / helped ...
Please feel free to cantact me. I would be happy to expand on my recommendation.

---

honest, dependable, and incredibly hardworking
Beyond that, she is an impressive problem solver who is able to address ...

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
Dear ..., 

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】开篇点题</mark>
- I am writing to share a recommendation that I believe will perfectly match your needs. **If I were forced to single out just one [ideal choice: candidate / destination / masterpiece] for** [Topic: the best tourist attraction / the most suitable candidate], **it would undoubtedly be** [Name: the Forbidden City / Mr. Li].
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】核心推荐理由</mark>
**推荐人物：**(逻辑链：从品质 -> 到能力 -> 到匹配度)
- **My confidence in recommending** [Name] **stems primarily from** his/her outstanding character. He/She **possesses** [品质: unyielding integrity and diligence], which are rare assets in today’s environment.
- **Complementing these personal attributes is** his/her impressive professional capability. **Featuring** [经历: extensive experience in student management], his/her resume proves that he/she is **an impressive problem solver**. **It is this unique combination of** character and competence **that makes him/her ideal for** the position.

---
**推荐地点：**(逻辑链：从整体地位 -> 到历史/自然 -> 到感官体验)
- **What distinguishes** [Place] **from other attractions is that it boasts** [特色: a perfect blend of history and nature]. **Located in** [地点], this spot enjoys a reputation as a "living museum."
- **Once you step into this place, you will find yourself steeped in** [history / nature]. **Walking through** [景点细节], visitors often feel like stepping into a different era. **Adding to its charm is** the local cuisine; the authentic dishes **will surely tantalize your taste buds and make your trip a culinary adventure.**

---
**推荐作品：**(逻辑链：从总体评价 -> 到具体内容 -> 到独特价值)
- **I would go so far as to say that**（我甚至可以说） [Book/Movie] **is a true masterpiece.** I thoroughly enjoyed my time reading it, and it **left a lasting impression on me**.
- **Specifically, the narrative features** [compelling storytelling], **allowing readers to** view the world from a fresh perspective. **Unlike other works that** merely scratch the surface, this one **has a very special spark**—it **captures the reader's heart** by revealing profound truths about [Topic].

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】再次感谢与期待</mark>
- **Given these reasons, I am convinced that** [Name/Item] **will exceed your expectations.**
- **Please feel free to contact me** should you require further details. **I would be more than happy to expand on my recommendation.**
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【底部】</mark>
Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
# 通知
[[2023年]]
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【顶部格式】</mark>
- **标题**：
	- 首行居中写标题
	- `Notice` / `Notice of [the Upcoming Lecture]` 
	- `Volunteers Needed for the City Museum` / `Call for Papers for the Academic Conference` / `Donations Needed for the Hope Project`
- **Current date**：居左写 eg: November 4, 2025

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第一段】写通知目的</mark>
- 开头：
	- 主办方开头：
		- [Host] is pleased to announce that...
			- **The Student Union is pleased to announce that** an English speech contest **will be held** in the main auditorium **at 2:00 p.m. on Friday, November 21st.** (学生会很高兴地宣布，一场英语演讲比赛将于11月21日（周五）下午2点在主礼堂举行。)
		- [Host] is organizing...
	- 事件开头：
		- A/An [Event] will be held ...
			- **A lecture on cross-cultural communication will be held** in Room 101, Teaching Building, from 7:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. on November 15th. (一场关于跨文化交际的讲座将于11月15日晚7点至9点在教学楼101室举行。)
		- Applications are now (or: currently) being accepted for...
			- **Applications are currently being accepted for** volunteer positions at the City Museum. （城市博物馆的志愿者岗位**现已开始接受申请**。）
		- [A research assitant] is sought to participate in [Activities: Prof. Smith's research projects concerning campus sports activities]. 
	- 受众开头：
		- All [Audience] are <mark style="background: #ADCCFFA6;">hereby</mark>（特此） notified that [...]
			- **All international students are hereby notified that** a welcome party will be organized on their behalf this Saturday. (特此通知所有国际学生：本周六将为你们举办一场欢迎派对。)
- 参加要求：
	- 必须参加：
		- （弱要求）And [all ...] are requested to [attend the event].
		- （强要求）Attendance is <mark style="background: #ADCCFFA6;">mandatory</mark>（强制性的） for all members.(所有成员必须出席。)
	- 自愿参加：
		- Volunteers are needed to [...]. 
		- We are now recruiting volunteers for...（我们正在为……招募志愿者。）
		- Interested [students] are encouraged / welcome to apply / participate.（欢迎所有感兴趣的同学参加。）

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第二段】</mark>
- 具体介绍：
	- [The event] aims to [...].
		- **The lecture aims to** provide students with a deeper understanding of cultural diversity. (本次讲座旨在为学生提供对文化多样性的更深理解。) 
	- [This event] is designed to [...].
		- **This initiative is designed to** foster a spirit of innovation on campus. (本活动旨在培养校园内的创新精神。)
- 要求：
	- Key activities will include ...
		- **Key activities will include** a keynote speech, a panel discussion, and a Q&A session.(主要活动将包括一场主旨演讲、一次小组讨论和一个问答环节。)
	- Applicants `are required to` / `should` possess ...
		- **Applicants should possess** strong communication skills and a passion for helping others. (申请人应具备强大的人际沟通能力和助人之情。)
	- Candidates must ...
- 优先条件：Priority and preference will be given to those who [...].
	- **Priority will be given to those who** have previous volunteering experience. (有志愿服务经验者将获优先考虑。)
- 福利：
	- [Complimentart meals / 50% discount] will be [provided / offered].
		- **Refreshments and free materials will be offered** to all attendees. (我们将为所有参与者提供茶点和免费资料。)
	- Participants will be provided with ...

<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">【第三段】响应通知的方式</mark>
- For those who wish to participate (or: register), please [做某事] no later than [截止日期].
	- **For those who wish to participate, please sign up** at the Student Union office **before this Friday, November 10th.** (有意参加者，请于本周五（11月10日）前在学生会办公室报名。)
---
- For further inquiries (or: information), please feel free to contact us at [邮箱/电话].
	- **For further inquiries, please feel free to contact** the organizing committee at [email@university.edu]. (如需进一步咨询，请随时联系组委会：[邮箱地址]。)
--- 
- We look forward to your active participation.
	- **We look forward to seeing you there.** (我们期待您的参与。)
- Your participation is warmly welcomed.
