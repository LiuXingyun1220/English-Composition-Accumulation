1. **Just as C is to D, so is A to B.**
	- Just as wings ==are== to a bird, so is practice to theory.
	- Just as constant dripping is to a hard stone, so is persistent effort to a **lofty** goal. A single drop is powerless, yet the **relentless** accumulation can overcome ant obstacle.
>[!note]
>- 用于关键论证：用在想强调的核心论点上
>用后必有解释：用一句话点明类比背后的逻辑，使论证更完整。
2. **A be among the starkest example of B**（A是B最鲜明（或最严酷）的例子之一。）
	- **The blind enthusiasm of many young people for Western festivals is among the starkest examples of the erosion of local cultural identity under the wave of globalization.** While celebrating foreign traditions can be a form of cultural exchange, a complete disregard for one's own heritage signals a deeper issue of cultural insecurity. （**许多年轻人对西方节日的盲目追捧，是全球化浪潮下本土文化身份流失最鲜明的例子之一。** 尽管庆祝外国传统可以是一种文化交流，但完全忽视自身传统则标志着一种更深层次的文化不安全感。）
>[!note]
>- **stark**  adj.（通常指不悦地）明显的
>- 需要一个**强有力的、甚至有些极端**的例子
3. **Nothing provokes more controversy than the suggestion that `[某个被理想化的事物A]` is anything less than `[对A的完美描述B]`.
	- **Nothing provokes more controversy than the suggestion that a university degree is anything less than a guaranteed path to a successful and fulfilling life.** In today's diverse society, vocational training and entrepreneurial spirit are increasingly recognized as equally valuable routes to personal achievement. （**没有什么比“大学文凭并非通往成功圆满人生的康庄大道”这种说法更能激起争议了。** 在当今多元化的社会中，职业培训和企业家精神正日益被承认为通往个人成就的同等重要的途径。）
4. X is as much about A as it is about B.（X 不仅关乎 B，也同样关乎 A。）
	- Innovation **is as much about** arduous trial and error **as it is about** a sudden flash of inspiration. (创新不仅是关于灵光一闪，**同样也是关于**艰苦卓绝的试错。)
	- True education **is as much about** cultivating character **as it is about** imparting knowledge. (真正的教育不仅在于传授知识，**同样也在于**塑造品格。)

5. The rise of **[某种社会现象]** has sparked a subtle war **between** **[具体对立面A]** and **[具体对立面B]** **— between [抽象对立面X] and [抽象对立面Y] —** whose outcome will **shape our collective future** in ways **we can only begin to imagine**.
	- The rise of Artificial Intelligence **has sparked a subtle war between** efficiency and humanity **— between** machine-driven calculation and humanistic care **—** whose outcome will **shape our collective future in ways we can only begin to imagine**. (人工智能的兴起，引发了一场效率与人性之间的微妙战争——亦即，一场机器驱动的计算与人文关怀之间的战争——其结果将以我们刚刚开始能够想象的方式，塑造我们的集体未来。)
