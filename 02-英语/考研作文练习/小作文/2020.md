# 题目
The Students Union of your university has assigned you to inform the international students about an upcoming singing contest. 

White a notice in about 100 words. 
Write your answer on the ANSWER SHEET. 
Do not use your own name in the notice. (10 points)

# 修改语法错误
## 修改后
**NOTICE** 
_December 20, 2025_

The Student Union is pleased to announce that a singing contest for international students will be held at the **University Auditorium** on January 1, 2026. All students are **welcome** to participate.

The contest is **designed** to enrich the **extracurricular activities** of our university and provide **stage experience** for candidates. Candidates should **possess** good singing skills and **be brave enough** to perform **in front of a large audience**. In addition, complimentary meals will be offered during the contest. 

Those who want to **participate** should sign up at Room 101, **Teaching Building**, before **next Friday**. Your participation will be **warmly welcomed**.

**The Student Union**
# 问题总结
| **错误单词/短语 (Original)** | **正确形式 (Correction)**          | **错误类型** | **记忆提示**                         |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------ | -------- | -------------------------------- |
| **posses**             | **possess**                    | 拼写错误     | 两个s，两个s (拥有双倍的s)                 |
| **speculators**        | **spectators** / audience      | 词义混淆     | Speculator是投机倒把的人；看表演的是Spectator |
| **extracurriculum**    | **extracurricular activities** | 形式错误     | 课外活动通常用复数短语                      |
| **teaching build**     | **Teaching Building**          | 单词残缺     | Building 不能缩写为 Build (动词)        |
| **warm welcomed**      | **warmly welcomed**            | 词性错误     | 修饰动词/分词要用副词 (ly)                 |
| **welcomed** (adj.)    | **welcome**                    | 词性错误     | "You are welcome" 不加d            |
| **designated** (to do) | **designed** / intended        | 词义辨析     | 旨在做某事用 designed                  |
