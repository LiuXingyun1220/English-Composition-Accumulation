1. **focal point**  n.问题的焦点，关注的核心  `main point, key issue`
	- 在引言段，引出文章的核心议题: While globalization facilitates cultural exchange on an unprecedented scale, the **focal point** of this interaction should not be the mere imitation of foreign trends, but the confident expression of our own cultural identity. (虽然全球化以前所未有的规模促进了文化交流，但这种交流的**核心焦点**不应仅仅是模仿外来潮流，而应是对我们自身文化身份的自信表达。)
	- 在论证段，作为段落主题句，提出分论点: Beyond the transmission of knowledge, the **focal point** of modern education must be the cultivation of critical thinking and innovation. This is what truly prepares students for the challenges of the future. (除了知识的传授，现代教育的**核心**必须是培养批判性思维和创新能力。这才是真正让学生为未来挑战做好准备的关键。)
	- 在分析原因或解决方案时，指出问题的症结: While technological solutions are often discussed, the true **focal point** of the environmental crisis lies in unsustainable human consumption patterns and a collective lack of long-term perspective. (尽管技术解决方案经常被讨论，但环境危机的真正**症结**在于不可持续的人类消费模式和集体长远眼光的缺失。)
	- 描述图表最显著的特征: As is vividly depicted in the line chart, the **focal point** is undoubtedly the sharp increase in the number of citizens choosing to travel by high-speed rail from 2015 to 2020, which soared by over 300%. (正如该线图生动描绘的那样，其**最引人注目的地方**无疑是2015年至2020年间选择乘坐高铁出行的市民数量的急剧增长，这一数字飙升了超过300%。)
