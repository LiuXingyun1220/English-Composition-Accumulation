# 3.上下同除以$\cos ^{n} x$，凑$\sec ^{2}x$
$$\begin{aligned}
I&=\int \frac{\tan x}{\tan x+1}=\int \frac{\tan x}{\tan x +1} \frac{\sec ^{2}x}{\sec ^{2}x}dx \\
&=\int \frac{\tan x}{\tan x+1} \frac{d\tan x}{1+\tan ^{2} x}=\int \frac{t}{1+t} \frac{1}{1+t^{2}}dt\\
\end{aligned}$$
留数法进行拆分，详情见【积累本】
# 4.头重脚轻法
$$\begin{aligned}
I&=\int \frac{\sin x}{\sqrt{ 2 }\sin\left( x+\frac{\pi}{4} \right)}dx=\int \frac{\sin \left( t-\frac{\pi}{4} \right)}{\sqrt{ 2 }\sin t}dt \\
&=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}\int \frac{\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}\sin t-\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}\cos t}{\sin t}dt=\frac{1}{2} \int\frac{\sin t-\cos t}{\sin t}dt\\
\end{aligned}$$
# 5.同乘因子分母变一项，分子分母大一统
$$
\begin{aligned}
I&=\int \frac{\sin x(\cos x-\sin x)}{\cos ^{2}x-\sin ^{2}x}dx=\int \frac{\sin x\cos x-\sin ^{2}x}{\cos 2x}dx\\
&=\int\frac{\frac{1}{2}\sin 2x-\frac{1}{2}(1-\cos 2x)}{\cos 2x}dx
\end{aligned}
$$

令$\tan \frac{x}{2}=t$，则$\sin x=\frac{2t}{1+t^{2}}$，$\cos x= \frac{1-t^{2}}{1+t^{2}}$，$dx=\frac{2}{1+t^{2}}dt$
