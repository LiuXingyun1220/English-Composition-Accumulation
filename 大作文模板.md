# 图画作文
## 首段（3-4句）
### 第1句：总体描述
【倒装1】
- `Portrayed`/`Depicted`/`Shown`in the above drawing is/are sth.
- `Portrayed`/`Depicted`/`Shown` in the drawing is `such a scene` / `an (striking/thought-provoking/eye-catching) scene`: [句子，首字母大写].
- ---
【倒装2】
- 短句：Before us lies an illuminating scene: [句子].
- 长句：Unfolding before us is an enlightening scene: [句子].
- ---
【主语从句】
- What is (vividly) depicted in this drawing is such a (symbolic) scene: [句子].
-  What is depicted in these drawings is `a stark contrast`: [句子,使用 while 或 whereas 连接的并列句].
---
【标准陈述句型】
- 短句：A remarkable and significant image comes into view: [句子].
- 长句：An extraordinary and meaningful drawing unfolds before our eyes: [句子].


### 第2-3句：图画细节描述
#### 1.在这个场景中，有一个【中心人物做着某事，这个中心人物是怎样的，与周边事物发生了什么，周边事物又是怎么样的】(单图)
- In/On+[地点]+sit(s)/lie(s)/stand(s) a/an+[中心人物，非谓语]. She/He/They/It [中心人物]+[v]+[周边事物].
- Sitting/Lying/Standing+in/on/in front of+[某地点]+is a/an+[中心人物],[非谓语], who...+[周边事物].
#### 2. 在一个大背景下，【主人公1】以什么样的方式做了什么，而【主人公2】又做了什么
- 大背景[非谓语]，[主1谓宾，非谓语1], while[主2谓宾，非谓语2]
### 第4句：文字说明
- The caption indicates/reads/says ...
- At the bottom of the drawing is the caption:...
- At the bottom of the picture, a sentece stands there prominently, and it says (that) ...
- At the bottom of the picture, a sentece stands there prominently, which can be briefly interpreted in this way:...
- At the bottom of the picture, there is an eye-catching sentece stands, and it says (that) ...
-  At the bottom of the picture, there is an eye-catching and thought-provoking sentece stands,which reads...


#### 2.[sth/主题词]这个被忽略的现象像达摩克里斯之剑一样悬在人类头上
- 短句：The overlooked phenomenon of [sth/主题词] hangs over humankind like the Sword of Damocles.
- 长句：
	- （非限定性从句+插入语+神话+定语从句）The phenomenon of [sth/主题词], which we tend to ignore, is the Sword of Damocles that still hangs over humankind.
	- (as引导让步状语倒装)Ignored as the phenomenon of [sth/主题词] may be, it is the Sword of Damocles that still hangs over humankind.
