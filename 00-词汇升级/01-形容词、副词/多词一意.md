	- **thought-provoking**  发人深省的；**profound**  深远的；**enlightening**  有启发性的；**illuminating** 富有启发性的
	- **stimulating**  趣味盎然的
	- **moving**  打动人的；**heratwarming**  暖人心扉的；**stirring**  激动人心的
	- **poignant**  令人悲痛的；**heart-wrenching**  令人心碎的
	- **evocative**  唤起回忆的
3. **许多的**
	- an abundance of +**可数 [C]** 或 **不可数 [U]**    （[C]用复数谓语动词 / [U]用单数）
		- There **is an abundance of** evidence supporting this theory. (有**大量的**证据支持这一理论。)
	- a myriad of + **可数 [C]** + **复数 (Plural)**
		- **Myriad** challenges **await** them in the future. (未来有**无数的**挑战等待着他们。) [`a myriad of` 也可以，但 `myriad + n.` 更简洁]
4. **一大群人** ^004d1b
	- 万金油：a multitude of +  [C] + 复数动词
	- 拥挤的：throngs of + 复数动词
	- 带贬义的：swarms of + [C] + 复数动词  （像蜂群）
5. **越来越...的**
	- **increasingly**
		- People are **increasingly** aware of the importance of environmental protection.
	- **progressively (逐渐地，日益)**
		- The situation has become **progressively** worse over the last decade. (在过去十年里，情况变得**越来越**糟。)
	- **steadily (稳步地，持续地)**
		- Life expectancy is **steadily** increasing due to better healthcare. (由于医疗水平的提高，预期寿命正在**持续**增长。)
	- **ever- (前缀构词法：愈发……的)**
		- **ever-increasing** (不断增长的): We must face the **ever-increasing** pressure of employment. (我们必须面对**日益增长的**就业压力。)
		- **ever-accelerating** (不断加速的)
		- **ever-worsening** (日益恶化的)
		- **ever-changing** (瞬息万变的): In this **ever-changing** world, adaptation is key. (在这个**瞬息万变的**世界里，适应是关键。)
6. **不停的，不断的**
	- <u>unremitting</u>（褒）
		- `unremitting efforts` (不懈的努力)
		- `unremitting attention` (持续的关注)
	- **relentless**（褒+贬）
		- `relentless pursuit of...` (对……的执着追求)
		- `relentless pressure / competition` (持续不断的压力/竞争)
	- **persistent**（褒+贬）
		- `persistent efforts` (持续努力)
		- `persistent problems` (顽疾)
